# Wool Sneakers E-commerce Site - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Core Concept: "Natural Wool Meets Urban Tech"
The design embodies the intersection of natural comfort and modern innovation. We're creating a premium shopping experience that feels both organic and sophisticated, appealing to conscious consumers who value sustainability without compromising on style or performance.

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Warm White (#F8F6F0) - Main background, representing pure wool
- Oat (#E8E0D0) - Secondary backgrounds and subtle accents
- Mist Grey (#C8C0B8) - Text and interface elements
- Charcoal (#4A4A4A) - Primary text and strong contrast elements

**Accent Colors:**
- Sage (#A8B0A0) - Low-saturation green for natural elements
- Stone (#B8A898) - Warm neutral for product highlights

### Typography
**Display Font:** Canela (serif) - For headlines and brand elements
- Elegant, sophisticated serif that conveys premium quality
- Used for hero headings, product names, and brand messaging

**Body Font:** Neue Haas Grotesk (sans-serif) - For all body text and UI
- Clean, modern sans-serif for optimal readability
- Used for descriptions, navigation, and interface elements

### Visual Language
**Texture & Materiality:**
- Subtle wool texture overlays
- Soft, organic shapes inspired by natural fibers
- Clean geometric forms for urban tech elements
- Gradient transitions that mimic the softness of wool

**Photography Style:**
- Natural lighting with soft shadows
- Minimal, clean compositions
- Focus on texture and material details
- Lifestyle shots in urban environments

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries & Effects
**Core Animation:** Anime.js for smooth micro-interactions
**Visual FX:** PIXI.js for wool texture backgrounds and particle effects
**Text Effects:** Typed.js for brand story reveals, Splitting.js for character animations
**Image Carousels:** Splide for product galleries
**Data Visualization:** ECharts for sustainability metrics

### Header & Hero Effects
- **Background:** Subtle wool texture animation using PIXI.js
- **Typography:** Split-by-letter reveal animations with staggered timing
- **Color Cycling:** Gentle color transitions on key brand messages
- **Parallax:** Minimal parallax scrolling for depth without distraction

### Interactive Elements
**Product Cards:**
- 3D tilt hover effects with soft shadows
- Smooth scale transitions on interaction
- Color-shifting borders that respond to wool-inspired gradients

**Navigation:**
- Clean, minimal design with subtle hover states
- Smooth transitions between sections
- Cart icon with bounce animation on item addition

**Buttons:**
- Soft, rounded corners inspired by organic shapes
- Gentle glow effects on hover
- Color transitions that echo natural wool tones

### Scroll Motion & Animations
**Reveal Animations:**
- Elements fade in with 16px upward movement
- Staggered timing for card grids (100ms delays)
- Smooth easing curves that feel natural

**Background Effects:**
- Subtle particle system representing wool fibers
- Gentle floating motion with physics-based movement
- Low-opacity overlays that don't interfere with content

### Product Showcase Styling
**Image Treatment:**
- Soft vignette effects on product photography
- Clean white backgrounds with subtle shadows
- Hover reveals showing alternate product angles

**Layout:**
- Generous white space for premium feel
- Grid systems that breathe with proper spacing
- Asymmetrical layouts that feel organic yet structured

### Mobile Considerations
- Touch-friendly interaction zones (44px minimum)
- Simplified animations that respect reduced motion preferences
- Optimized image loading with progressive enhancement
- Maintained visual hierarchy across all screen sizes

## Brand Integration
**Logo Treatment:** Clean, minimal wordmark in Canela serif
**Iconography:** Custom icons inspired by wool fibers and urban elements
**Loading States:** Subtle wool-inspired animations during content loading
**Error States:** Gentle, helpful messaging with natural imagery
